The concept

World Golf Challenge is the first digital golf platform bringing together all levels of amateurs players around the world – to compete in virtual Challenges online while playing on real courses.

Join the Challenge of your choice

You can play the week challenge, the saturday or sunday challenges, or some special event challenges !

Go out, play golf

Once you joined a challenge, it's time to earn some points.

Enter your score in your Lobby

You must enter your score on the same day you played the course.

Get your prize !

At the end of the tournament, check your results, and if you are on the podium, claim your prize.

How to play

Chose a Challenge matching your real round of golf. For Buy-in, use our secured partner PayPal to credit your account. Prize pool breakdown will be visible in your Challenge Lobby. Amateurs can join a Challenge of a higher handicap categories but not lower. Pros don’t play Amateurs Challenges (except Private).

2
Enter your score

Once your round is over, go back to your Lobby (green bottom line on your mobile, green box bottom left on your desktop) and enter your score of the day. You can use your score for several Challenges. Make sure to enter your score at the requested format (stroke play or stableford, etc.). Once your score confirmed, you will discover your provisional ranking.

3
Leaderboard

Your ranking is visible only when your score is entered. The Challenge final ranking and breakdown will be displayed once the Challenge is over (10pm CET). Find out your rankings in your profile section (picture) or in the Leaderboard section (except Private).
