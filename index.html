How to play

Chose a Challenge matching your real round of golf. For Buy-in, use our secured partner PayPal to credit your account. Prize pool breakdown will be visible in your Challenge Lobby. Amateurs can join a Challenge of a higher handicap categories but not lower. Pros don’t play Amateurs Challenges (except Private).

Chose a Challenge on the screen, go play on the greens. For Buy-in, use our secured partner PayPal to credit your account. All infos will be visible in your Challenge Lobby.

Enter your score

You must enter your score the same day before 10pm CET in your Lobby - green section. Make sure to enter your score at the correct format (i.e. Net Stableford vs Net Stroke Play).

3
Leaderboard

The Challenge final ranking and breakdown will be displayed once the Challenge is over (10pm CET). Find out your rankings in your profile section or in the Leaderboard section (except Private).
